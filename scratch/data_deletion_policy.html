User Data Deletion Policy

At Social Suite, we value your privacy and provide you with full control over your personal data. This policy outlines how you can request the deletion of your account and associated information.

How to Request Data Deletion

You can request the deletion of your data through the following methods:

  • In-App Deletion: Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account. This will automatically queue your data for permanent deletion.
  • Email Request: Send an email to info@brainket.com with the subject "Data Deletion Request". Please include your registered email address or phone number.
  • Contact Form: You can also contact us via our website at brainket.com.

What Data is Deleted?

Upon a successful deletion request, we will remove the following information from our active databases:

  • Personal profile information (Name, Email, Phone).
  • Business profiles and configurations.
  • Social media account connections and access tokens.
  • AI-generated content and history.
  • Usage logs and preferences.

Data Retention

Please note that some information may be retained in our backup archives for a limited period (up to 30 days) to prevent fraud, comply with legal obligations, or troubleshoot technical issues. After this period, the data is permanently purged.
